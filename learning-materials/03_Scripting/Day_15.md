# Day 15: Iterable Objects (Lists, Tuples, Dictionaries)

## 🧠 Key Concepts
Iterable objects hold multiple values.
* **Tuple:** Ordered, unchangeable (uses `()`).
* **List:** Ordered, changeable (uses `[]`).
* **Dictionary:** Unordered, changeable, indexed by key/value pairs (uses `{}`).
