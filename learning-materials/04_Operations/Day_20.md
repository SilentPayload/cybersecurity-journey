# Day 20: Vulnerability Management Lifecycle

## 🧠 Key Concepts
The process of identifying and remediating weaknesses before they are exploited.


[Image of vulnerability management lifecycle]


**The Lifecycle:**
1. **Discover:** Asset identification.
2. **Scan:** Running automated vulnerability scans (e.g., Nessus).
3. **Analyze:** Differentiating false positives from real threats.
4. **Remediate:** Patching or implementing compensating controls.
5. **Verify:** Rescanning to ensure the fix worked.
6. **Report:** Documenting for compliance and stakeholders.
