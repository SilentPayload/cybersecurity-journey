# Day 24: SIEM Analysis & Alerting

## 🧠 Key Concepts
How to investigate an alert:
1. **Analyze:** What triggered the alert?
2. **Filter:** Remove "noise" (false positives).
3. **Timeline:** Reconstruct the events leading to the alert.
4. **Decision:** Is this a security incident or benign activity?
