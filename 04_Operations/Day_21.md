# Day 21: Prioritization & Remediation

## 🧠 Key Concepts
Not all vulnerabilities are equal. We must prioritize based on risk.
* **CVE (Common Vulnerabilities and Exposures):** A unique identifier for a specific vulnerability (e.g., CVE-2023-XXXX).
* **CVSS (Common Vulnerability Scoring System):** A standardized system to score the severity of vulnerabilities.
    * 0.0 - 10.0 scale.
    * Helps focus resources on "Critical" and "High" risks first.
