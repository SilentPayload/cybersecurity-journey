# Day 30: IR Playbooks

## 🧠 Key Concepts
* **Playbooks:** Step-by-step procedures for specific incident types (Phishing, Ransomware, DDoS).
* **Roles & Responsibilities:** Who does what? (Who isolates the machine? Who notifies the legal team?).
* **Standardization:** Ensuring consistent response regardless of who is on shift.
