# Day 26: Threat Detection & Hunting

## 🧠 Key Concepts
* **Threat Detection (Reactive):** Finding attacks based on alerts or suspicious activity.
* **Threat Hunting (Proactive):** Actively searching for hidden attacks that haven't triggered alerts yet.
* **MITRE ATT&CK Framework:** A globally accessible knowledge base of adversary tactics and techniques. We use this to map real-world actions to known attacker behaviors.
