# Day 29: Incident Response (IR) Lifecycle

## 🧠 Key Concepts
The structured process to identify, contain, and recover from cybersecurity incidents.



**The 6 Phases:**
1. **Preparation:** Policies, training, backups, and playbooks.
2. **Detection & Analysis:** Confirming the incident and scoping the impact.
3. **Containment:** Stopping the spread (e.g., isolating a machine).
4. **Eradication:** Removing the root cause (e.g., deleting malware).
5. **Recovery:** Restoring services safely.
6. **Lessons Learned:** Post-incident review to improve security.
