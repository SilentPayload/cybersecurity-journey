# Day 23: Intro to SIEM

## 🧠 Key Concepts
**SIEM (Security Information & Event Management):** Centralized log management and analytics.


* **Data Sources:** Firewalls (blocked/allowed), Servers (login attempts), Endpoints (AV alerts), IDS/IPS (malware).
* **Core Functions:**
    * **Log Collection:** Gathering data from the entire network.
    * **Correlation:** Detecting patterns across different logs.
    * **Alerting:** Notifying analysts of suspicious activity.
    * **Dashboards:** Visualizing security posture.
