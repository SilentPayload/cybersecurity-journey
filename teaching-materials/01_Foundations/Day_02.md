# Day 2: Risk Management & Threat Intelligence

## 🧠 Key Concepts
### Risk Management
**Formula:** Risk = Threat × Vulnerability × Impact (or Likelihood × Impact)
* **Threats:** Hackers, malware, insiders.
* **Vulnerabilities:** Weak passwords, outdated software.
* **Impact:** Financial loss, downtime, legal issues.

**Strategies:**
1. **Avoidance:** Don't take the risk.
2. **Mitigation:** Reduce the risk.
3. **Transfer:** Insurance/Outsourcing.
4. **Acceptance:** Live with it if the impact is low.

### Threat Intelligence (TI)
Information that helps predict, prevent, and respond to threats.
* **Strategic TI:** Big picture (executives).
* **Tactical TI:** Specific TTPs (SOC analysts).
* **Operational TI:** Real-time campaigns/malware details (Incident Responders).
* **Sources:** OSINT, Threat feeds, Dark web.
