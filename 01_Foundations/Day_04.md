# Day 4: Asset Security & Data Controls

## 🧠 Key Concepts
### Asset Identification
If you don't know what your assets are, you cannot protect them.
* **Types:** Data, Devices (IoT/Laptops), Users, Systems (Apps/OS).

### Data Classification
1. **Public:** No risk to share.
2. **Internal:** Employees only.
3. **Confidential:** Authorized users only.
4. **Restricted:** Highly sensitive.

### Security Controls
Defenses to protect assets: Encryption, Access Control, Data Loss Prevention (DLP).
