# Day 3: OSINT Lab

## 🧠 Key Concepts
**OSINT (Open Source Intelligence):** Gathering public information about a person or organization.
* **Basic Tools:** Whois, Wayback Machine, crt.sh (Subdomains), Whatsmyname.

## 📝 OSINT Report Template
* **Target/Org/Analyst/Date**
* **Executive Summary:** Brief overview.
* **Introduction:** Objective, Scope, Methodology.
* **Findings:** Digital footprint, social media, domains.
* **Analysis:** Interpreting patterns and anomalies.
* **Conclusion & Recommendations:** Actionable next steps.
