# Day 5: Legal & Regulatory Compliance

## 🧠 Key Concepts
Regulations protect privacy, ensure accountability, and set security standards.

### Key Regulations
* **GDPR (Europe):** Data privacy, 72-hour breach notification, heavy fines.
* **HIPAA (US Healthcare):** Protecting PHI (Protected Health Information).
* **PCI-DSS:** Credit/debit card data protection.

### Case Studies
* **British Airways (GDPR):** Inadequate security measures = major fines.
* **Anthem (HIPAA):** Lack of encryption on healthcare data.
* **Target (PCI-DSS):** Poor vendor management (HVAC credentials compromised).
