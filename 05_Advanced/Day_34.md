# Day 34: Digital Forensics

## 🧠 Key Concepts
The science of identifying, preserving, and analyzing digital evidence.


* **Golden Rules:** Never work on original evidence, document everything, and respect the chain of custody.
* **Chain of Custody:** The chronological log of who handled the evidence. Vital for legal cases.
* **Forensic Triage:** A rapid examination to preserve critical evidence during an incident.
* **Disk Imaging:** Creating bit-for-bit copies (Raw/dd, E01, AFF) to ensure data integrity.
