# Day 33: Identity & Access Management (IAM)

## 🧠 Key Concepts
IAM ensures the right people access the right resources at the right time.


* **AuthN vs. AuthZ:** Authentication (Who are you?) vs. Authorization (What can you do?).
* **Access Control Models:**
    * **DAC:** Owner decides access.
    * **MAC:** Centralized mandatory policies.
    * **RBAC:** Access via roles.
    * **ABAC:** Access via attributes (location, time, device).
* **Modern Security:** Least Privilege, MFA, and SSO (Single Sign-On).
