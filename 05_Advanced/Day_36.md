# Day 36: Career Development & Portfolio Polish

## 🧠 Key Concepts

* **The "Elevator Pitch":** Practice summarizing your 36-day journey in 60 seconds.
* **Next Steps:** Consider certifications (CompTIA Security+, BTL1) to validate what you’ve learned.
